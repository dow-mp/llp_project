Love Local Parks is...

dedicated to cleaning up our community parks and playgrounds for the enjoyment of everyone.

I started Love Local Parks because bringing my young child to play and seeing my local playground strewn with trash made me really sad. I realized I could do something about this, and it wouldn't even be that hard.

I started picking up the trash and throwing it away. It was that simple. And, it made me feel better.

Picking up the trash at my local park made the park better and made me feel happy to spend time there.

I want all of us to enjoy these spaces and be happy to spend time outdoors. We can! It's so easy to grab a trash bag, a pair of gloves, and get outside to help out at our local park.

We love local parks.
